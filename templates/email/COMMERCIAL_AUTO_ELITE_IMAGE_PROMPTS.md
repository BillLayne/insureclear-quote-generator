# Commercial Auto Elite Gmail Image Prompts

Use these prompts to create two realistic images for `COMMERCIAL_AUTO_ELITE_QUOTE_SAMPLE.html`.

## 1. Hero Image Prompt

Create a realistic, professional commercial auto insurance hero image for a North Carolina independent insurance agency email template. Show two clean business vehicles parked outside a small-town service business: one white Ford Transit-style service van and one white or silver contractor pickup truck. The vehicles should look like real insured business vehicles, not luxury cars, and should be lightly branded with generic small-business markings only, no readable company name. The scene should feel trustworthy, local, and practical, with soft daylight, a clean storefront or workshop in the background, and a subtle small-town North Carolina feel. Composition should be wide horizontal, email-header friendly, with the vehicles centered and enough space around them so faces or important details are not cropped. Style should be photorealistic, warm, premium but not flashy, natural colors, high detail, no cartoon style, no text overlay, no logos, no watermarks.

Recommended format:
- Aspect ratio: 16:9 or 3:2
- Size: at least 1200 x 675
- Best email crop: important subjects centered in the middle 70 percent
- Avoid: readable business names, license plate numbers, distorted wheels, fake insurance papers, busy backgrounds

## 2. Inside Agent Consultation Image Prompt

Create a realistic, friendly insurance agency consultation image for a commercial auto quote email. Show a local male insurance agent in a business-casual outfit sitting at a desk with a small-business owner, reviewing commercial vehicle coverage. On the desk are simple paperwork, a tablet or laptop showing a blurred vehicle list, and a coffee cup. Through a nearby window or glass door, a work van or pickup can be softly visible outside. The mood should be warm, local, helpful, and professional, like an independent agent explaining coverage in plain English. The agent should look approachable and trustworthy, not corporate or staged. Use natural indoor lighting, warm neutral tones, and a clean office setting. Photorealistic style, no text overlay, no visible brand logos, no readable documents, no watermarks.

Recommended format:
- Aspect ratio: 16:9 or 3:2
- Size: at least 1200 x 675
- Best email crop: people centered with heads fully visible
- Avoid: fake smiles, distorted hands, readable personal information, cluttered desks, dark lighting
