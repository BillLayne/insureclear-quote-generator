Coverage Details

Your Coverage Levels.

Click any coverage to learn more

Dwelling coverage pays to repair or rebuild the physical structure of your home (including the roof, walls, foundation, and fixtures) if it is damaged by a covered disaster like a fire, windstorm, lightning, or vandalism.

Calculated replacement cost to ensure full structural protection.

This coverage pays for damage to unattached structures on your property. This includes items such as a detached garden shed, property line fences, detached garages, or guest cottages. Standard policies set this at 10% of your Coverage A limit.

Personal Property coverage reimburses you for the value of your furniture, clothes, electronics, appliances, and other personal items if they are damaged, stolen, or destroyed by a covered loss.

Note: High-value personal items like jewelry or fine art may require scheduled coverage riders. Ask us for details.

If your home is rendered uninhabitable due to a covered claim, Loss of Use pays for additional living expenses. This covers hotel costs, rental homes of equivalent size, and restaurant meal expenses while your home is being repaired.

Personal Liability protects you and members of your household against lawsuits for bodily injury or property damage caused to other people. It covers legal defense bills, court judgments, and settlements up to your policy limit.

This pays for necessary medical expenses for guests who are accidentally injured on your property, regardless of who is legally at fault for the accident.
