# Commercial Auto Webpage Image Prompts

Use these prompts when replacing the temporary commercial auto images in `COMMERCIAL_AUTO_QUOTE_MASTER_TEMPLATE.html`.

## 1. Hero Background Image

Create a realistic, premium commercial auto insurance webpage hero background for a small North Carolina business. Show two clean business vehicles, such as a white service van and a contractor pickup truck, parked outside a professional small-town business or workshop. The vehicles should look practical and work-ready, not luxury or personal-use vehicles. Keep branding generic with no readable company names, no license plate details, and no text overlay. The image should feel local, trustworthy, bright, and professional, with soft daylight and a clean business setting. Compose it wide so the vehicles sit mostly in the center-right area, leaving darker open space on the left for webpage headline text and a pricing card overlay. Photorealistic, natural colors, polished but not corporate, no logos, no watermarks, no distorted wheels or fake text.

Recommended:
- Aspect ratio: 16:9 or wider
- Size: at least 1800 x 1000
- Important subjects: center-right
- Leave safe empty/darker space on the left for text overlay
- Avoid: readable signage, license plates, brand logos, cartoons, overly dark background

## 2. Inside Quote Page Image

Create a realistic photo for the inside section of a commercial auto insurance quote webpage. Show a friendly local male insurance agent reviewing business auto coverage with a small-business owner at a desk. On the desk, include a laptop or tablet with a blurred vehicle list, car keys, and simple paperwork. Through a window or glass door, a work van or pickup truck should be softly visible outside. The mood should be warm, clear, and consultative, showing that a real person checks the details before anything is submitted. Photorealistic, natural indoor light, clean office, approachable expressions, professional but not staged. No readable documents, no visible private information, no text overlay, no logos, no watermarks.

Recommended:
- Aspect ratio: 16:9 or 3:2
- Size: at least 1400 x 900
- Keep faces fully visible
- Keep the scene bright enough for webpage use
- Avoid: clutter, fake smiles, distorted hands, unreadable messy screens, dark lighting
