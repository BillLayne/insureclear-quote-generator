What's the difference between replacement cost and actual cash value?

Replacement cost rebuilds or replaces at today's prices with no deduction for age or wear. Actual cash value subtracts depreciation, so you'd get less. This quote is written on a {{DWELLING_SETTLEMENT}} basis for your dwelling and a {{PROPERTY_SETTLEMENT}} basis for your belongings.

How does my wind and hail deductible work?

For storm damage from wind or hail, your deductible is {{WIND_HAIL_FAQ}}. Every other type of covered claim uses your {{ALL_PERIL_DEDUCTIBLE}} all-other-perils deductible. This is common for North Carolina homes.

What about my mortgage company?

If your home has a mortgage, we list your lender on the policy and send them proof of insurance automatically. If they pay your insurance through escrow, we coordinate the billing with them so nothing slips through the cracks.
