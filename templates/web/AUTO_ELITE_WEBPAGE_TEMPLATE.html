Good questions

Before you say yes

What happens to my current policy?

We help time your cancellation to the new effective date so there is no gap, no overlap, and no lapse on your record. If you are owed a refund on unused premium, your old carrier sends it directly to you.

When do I get my new ID cards?

Digital ID cards usually arrive the same business day you accept. If you have a lienholder, we notify them and send proof of insurance automatically.

What if I add a driver or swap a vehicle later?

One call, text, or email to us and we can handle a new vehicle, new driver, address change, or coverage adjustment. That is the point of having a local agent.
